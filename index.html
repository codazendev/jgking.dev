Projects
  • Travelling with Taste: A 3D puzzle-platformer where you play as a bunch of rats in a suitcase. Your goal is to navigate an airport baggage handling area and make it to the plane before it takes off! Along the way you must avoid sinister security guards and collect as much cheese as you can. Developed in Unreal Engine over the course of seven weeks in collaboration with seven total team members.
